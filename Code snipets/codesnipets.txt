 <section class="section-container fade-in first-info">
    <h2>The River Ridge Royal Robotics team is a dynamic group of young men and women working together to meet team goals, which are:</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>To develop skills and experiences that will prepare them for college and their careers.</li>
      <li>To promote appreciation for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).</li>
    </ul>
    <p>Through participation in Royal Robotics, students are able to:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Develop critical thinking and trouble-shooting abilities</li>
      <li>Practice STEM skills</li>
      <li>Learn leadership, communication, and collaboration skills</li>
      <li>Gain engineering experience and have hands-on practice building/programming robots</li>
      <li>Compete in FIRST Robotics Competitions throughout Florida and the United States</li>
      <li>Increase college and career opportunities</li>
      <li>Participate in community outreach activities</li>
    </ul>
    </section>
