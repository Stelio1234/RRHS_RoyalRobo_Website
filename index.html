About Us

River Ridge High School's Royal Robotics team is dedicated to inspiring the next generation of engineers, programmers, and innovators. Since 2015, we've competed in the FIRST Robotics Competition with passion, teamwork, and excellence.

Our Mission

To empower students through hands-on robotics challenges, foster STEM education, and build leaders who create meaningful impact in their communities and beyond.
